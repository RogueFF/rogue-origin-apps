Order Photos — Quick Button

A one-click button for your bookmarks bar. While viewing any order in Shopify, click it and the order's packed-photos open automatically — no typing.

📷 Order Photos
↑ Drag this button up to your bookmarks bar.
(Don't click it here — it only works on a Shopify order page.)

How to use it

  1. Drag the gold button above onto your browser's bookmarks bar (if the bar is hidden, press Ctrl/⌘ + Shift + B to show it).
  2. Open an order in Shopify admin.
  3. Click 📷 Order Photos in your bookmarks bar. It reads the order # off the page, lets you confirm it, then opens that order's photos in a new tab.
